Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. This service ("Smurf Detector") does not store, save, or collect any personal information about you.

When you perform a search, your request (Riot ID and Region) is sent to our secure backend function on Vercel. This function then calls the official Riot Games API to retrieve the requested data. We do not log or store this data. The data is simply passed from the Riot API to you.

This website does not use cookies or any tracking technologies.
